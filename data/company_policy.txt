TECHCORP CUSTOMER SUPPORT POLICY DOCUMENT
Version 2.1 | Last Updated: January 2026

===========================================
SECTION 1: REFUND POLICY
===========================================

TechCorp offers a 30-day money back guarantee on all products.
Customers can request a full refund within 30 days of purchase.
After 30 days, partial refunds of 50% are available up to 60 days.
No refunds are available after 60 days from purchase date.

To request a refund, customers must:
1. Contact support at support@techcorp.com
2. Provide order number and reason for refund
3. Allow 5-7 business days for processing

Premium plan customers receive extended 60-day full refund policy.
Enterprise customers have custom refund terms in their contracts.

===========================================
SECTION 4: DATA PRIVACY
===========================================

TechCorp complies with GDPR and CCPA regulations.
Customer data is encrypted at rest and in transit.
Data is stored in AWS US-East region by default.
EU customers data is stored in AWS EU-West region.
Data retention period is 7 years for billing records.
Personal data can be deleted upon request within 30 days.
TechCorp never sells customer data to third parties.
Annual security audits are conducted by independent firms.
